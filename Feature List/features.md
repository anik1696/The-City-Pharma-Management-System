# The City Pharma - Feature List

## 1. Advanced Point of Sale (POS)
* **Smart Search:** Search medicines by brand name, generic name, or barcode.
* **Dynamic Cart:** Easily adjust quantities, remove items, and view real-time totals including VAT calculations.
* **Customer Assignment:** Link sales to registered customers to track loyalty points, or quickly process as a "Walk-in".
* **Flexible Payments:** Support for multiple payment methods including Cash, Card, and Mobile Money (bKash, Nagad).
* **Automated Receipts:** Generates digital invoices immediately upon sale completion.

## 2. Interactive Dashboard
* **Real-Time KPIs:** Instant view of today's revenue, customer count, and critical low-stock alerts.
* **Visual Analytics:** Interactive "Weekly Revenue Overview" bar charts tracking financial performance over the last 7 days.
* **Role-Based Views:** Managers see global statistics, while Staff members see their personalized "Shift Summary".
* **Stock Overview:** Quick visual breakdown of current stock by category (Tablets, Capsules, Syrups, Injections).

## 3. Comprehensive Inventory Management
* **Detailed Catalog:** Track complete medicine details including Brand, Generic Name, Manufacturer, and Category.
* **Stock Tracking:** Real-time stock levels with automated "Low Stock" and "Out of Stock" warnings based on custom thresholds.
* **Safety & Compliance:** Track Batch Numbers and Expiry Dates to prevent the sale of expired medication.
* **Easy Adjustments:** Quickly add or remove stock when new shipments arrive or damaged goods are removed.

## 4. Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
* **Customer Directory:** Securely store customer contact information, addresses, and date of birth.
* **Loyalty Program:** Automatically track customer purchase history and loyalty points for future discounts.
* **Medical Notes:** Keep secure records of specific customer medical notes or prescription requirements.

## 5. Reports & Analytics
* **Sales Reports:** Detailed tabular data breaking down daily revenue, number of invoices, and average sale value.
* **Revenue Breakdown:** Visual stacked charts showing revenue distribution by payment method.
* **Inventory Reports:** Instant lists of medicines hitting low stock thresholds or expiring within 60 days.

## 6. Staff Management
* **Staff Directory:** Manage employee profiles, contact details, and assigned shifts (Morning, Afternoon, Evening).
* **Role Management:** Differentiate access levels between Managers (full access) and Staff (restricted access).
* **Performance Tracking:** Track sales and revenue generated by individual staff members.

## 7. System Settings & Configuration
* **Business Profile:** Customize pharmacy name, address, and receipt footer messages.
* **Tax & Billing:** Configure custom VAT rates, currency symbols, and global discount toggles.
* **Security & Alerts:** Set custom session timeouts, audit logging, and expiry alert thresholds.

## 8. Premium User Experience (UI/UX)
* **Modern Interface:** Oracle-inspired top navigation with a dynamic, organic hero section.
* **Day & Night Modes:** Fully integrated Light/Cream and Dark modes to reduce eye strain.
* **Responsive Design:** Fluid layouts with micro-animations and non-intrusive toast notifications.
